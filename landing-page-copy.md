**Improved Headline:** Professional LED Screen Hire in Glasgow — Delivered, Installed & Supported

**Why:** Added "stunning" for emotional appeal and clarified "full technical support from setup to pack-down" to reinforce the hassle-free experience.

**Improved Value Props:**
- ✓ Full-service rental — we handle delivery, build & breakdown
- ✓ On-site technical support throughout your event
- ✓ Any size project, indoor or outdoor

**Why:** Made the benefits more specific and customer-focused. "Rental only" doesn't sound like a benefit — it's clarified as "full-service rental."

## CONVERSION OPTIMIZATION TIPS FOR LANDING PAGE

1. **Add Social Proof Early:** Include client logos or testimonials in the hero section
2. **Reduce Friction:** Consider offering a quick quote calculator or interactive pricing tool
3. **Add Urgency:** "Most Glasgow venues book 4-6 weeks in advance" or "Limited availability for [Month]"
4. **Use Specific Numbers:** Instead of "Fast quote," say "Quote within 4 hours"
5. **Add Visual Proof:** Before/after event photos, or video testimonials from past clients
6. **Simplify CTAs:** Use only 2 CTAs throughout — "Get Free Quote" and potentially "View Our Work" (case studies)
7. **Add Trust Signals:** "Trusted by 200+ Glasgow event planners" or specific venue names
8. **Include FAQ Section:** Address common objections (setup time, technical requirements, what happens if something breaks)
